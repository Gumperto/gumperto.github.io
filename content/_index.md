## Introduction

This is the website of Gumperto. This is where I post about my process of working on various programming projects (game-making, tsnp, etc) or learning various things (Japanese), my personal thoughts on things from AI Safety to mildly trashy light novels, my fiction (original and fanmade), among other things.

Use the navigation bar at the top of the website to navigate around the site.

## How things work

Posts on this website work off a update-as-I-go and finish-as-I-feel-like basis.
It's still a very young site, so I expect there to be a lot more of the former,
but I do like the idea of having a in-progress indicator like there is with [gwern.net](https://gwern.net/)
in case I get into the idea of making some perennial content.

I don't have a set release schedule, as I am working on the site in my own
free time. I'll go and implement an RSS feed when it makes sense.
